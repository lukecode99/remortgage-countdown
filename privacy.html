Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data We Collect

Your mortgage details are stored on your device only and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

Mortgage Rate Data

The app fetches current UK mortgage rates from an anonymous public endpoint. This request contains no personal information — your device's IP address reaches the server, but no other identifying data is sent or logged.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled locally on your device using iOS notifications. No push token or device identifier is sent to our servers.

Third-party Services

This app uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
